About

  • BEGINNING=POSSIBILITIES

    To commemorate my journey, which began 13 years ago when a post-graduation dropout full of enthusiasm and heat within wanted to explore entrepreneurship in a bid to create a unique identity for herself. Call it mystic, but my first entrepreneurial venture started with a wrong phone call from a consultant which led me to meet my first employer. Together, we worked on a concept and strived hard for its success. It took a lot of time and hard work in the evolving and fast pacing competitive education industry to make our mark. But our consistent hard work did lead us to success, and the business was witnessing rapid growth. We were a great team and knew we could do something great together.

  • CHALLENGES=GROWTH

    My first break as an entrepreneur was when I turned into a business partner for the project. Since then, we have been through several ups and downs and instances where I really had to struggle to keep myself motivated. But over the years, being an entrepreneur has been a rewarding journey, and I am now contributing my significance in GlobalEdugate, the Aggregator platform in Ed Tech Industry. There is no perfect timing to do something which contributes towards social benefit and parallels, I feel good to contribute towards the empowerment of youth by advising them during the early stage, and other startups in agrotech & service-based industries. I am a mentor to “Farmers City” & supporting & mentoring other startups to uplift through an initiative called “Startup Stairs”.

  • ASSOCIATIONS=PROSPERITY

    I strongly believe in collaborating and working, I am actively collaborating with a number of people in my professional network, for a few I am the advisor & mentor, and for many, I am providing the growth hacking platform to help their brand expansion and excel on sustainable & scalable business models.

  • SHARING=ABUNDANCE

    Over the years, I have also realized the importance of sharing my experiences with the youth to help them grow as an entrepreneur, and more importantly, as an individual. With this goal in mind, I created this website and put in all my learning and thoughts to share with the young aspiring entrepreneurial minds.

  • CREATING=VALUE

    My book “A Woman Unparalleled” talks about a host of common problems that millennials of India commonly struggle with within their personal and professional lives. I have tried to share the valuable learnings of my entrepreneurial journey with budding entrepreneurial minds.
